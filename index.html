Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

Located in the heart of Titabar, HOT TANDOOR is not just a restaurant; it's a culinary destination. We have built our reputation on serving the most authentic Tandoori delicacies, aromatic Biryanis, and savory Chinese cuisine in Assam.

Every dish is a masterpiece, prepared with fresh ingredients, traditional spices, and a passion for perfection. Whether you crave the smoky char of our Tandoor or the comfort of a hot bowl of noodles, we promise an unforgettable dining experience in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere.
